Junior Development


       I love working with children. Watching them grow and develope their tennis game is one of the simple rewards that life gives me.

Requirements

Please note that if your child is under the age of 6, you must be present the whole time.
  1. Age: Although there are no techincal age requirements, I do require a trial lesson to asses that they are ready for lessons.
  2. Racket: This depends on your child's height. When picking a racket, make sure that the handle is no higher than 1/2 way between the knee and hip. If you're an inbetween size, go for the larger racket.
  3. Shoes: Please bring athletic shoes. Flat soled shoes or sandals can be a potetial risk for injury due to falling. (please note that all tennis courts require non-marking shoes)
  4. WATER! Many parents forget water and hydration is key especially on hot days.
