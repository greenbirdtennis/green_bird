Adult overview

Come out for some fun exercise!

I understand that you've probably been sitting in the office all day and want to go out and get some exercise. Tennis is a great solution! Tennis a cardio work out, core intensive, and social all in one. Wether you are a recreational player or a tournament player, I can be your consistent hitting partner, trainer, and friend.

With the understanding of the adult game, I understand there are worries about injuries. This goes with my tennis tip #3. Relax your grip! Relaxing your grip will loosen your muscles in your wrist and forearm allowing them to be flexible. The muscles in your wrist and forearm are smaller and weaker which makes them prone to injury. Allowing them to be flexible, they are the end to your arm's kinetic chain makeing them the crack of the whip of your swing while your upper body and shoulders stay quiet.
